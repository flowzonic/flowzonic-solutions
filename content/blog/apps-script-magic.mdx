Automation is the secret weapon of the modern enterprise. At Flowzonic, we've seen businesses transform overnight simply by removing the manual friction from their daily operations.

## The Power of Google Apps Script

Most companies use less than 10% of what Google Workspace is actually capable of. With Apps Script, we can bridge the gap between Sheets, Docs, and Gmail to create a seamless, self-driving ecosystem.

### What We Can Automate

- **Automated Reporting**: No more manual data entry for weekly meetings.
- **Gmail Workflows**: Automatically sort, summarize, and reply to leads.
- **Dynamic PDF Generation**: Create invoices or reports instantly from a spreadsheet row.

## Case Study: Reclaiming 20 Hours a Week

We recently worked with a logistics firm that was manually tracking shipments in a shared sheet. By implementing a custom script that pulled data directly from their carrier API, we reclaimed **20 hours per week** for their operations lead.
